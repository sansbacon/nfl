8 Matthew Stafford DET Q vs. GB 2 20 7.8 2.1
Stafford is a must-start this week against Green Bay with the season on the line. He should throw the ball a ton and the Packers have a weak secondary.
9 Jameis Winston TB vs. CAR 5 18 9.6 1.7
The Bucs have a huge implied total this week and Doug Martin is out, so that means Vegas is expecting big things from Jameis and fantasy owners should too.
10 Cam Newton CAR Q at TB 5 14 9.9 1.6
Cam has over a 50% completion rate just once since Week 11 and isn't rushing for many yards. Even against Tampa's defense, he is a very risky start.
11 Blake Bortles JAC Q at IND 6 20 11.1 2.4
Bortles had a big week against the abysmal Titans' secondary last week and draws another weak defense in Week 17, so he can be started but there are probably better options.
12 Philip Rivers LAC vs. KC 7 20 12.6 2.2
Kansas City does have a great fantasy defense, but mostly because they force loads of turnovers. The Chiefs give up plenty of yards and TDs so don't be scared off by this matchup.
13 Carson Palmer ARI Q at LAR 6 27 12.9 2.8
The Rams get after the quarterback and Palmer as taken as many sacks as almost every QB in the league and is a turnover machine so it's best to avoid him in this matchup.
